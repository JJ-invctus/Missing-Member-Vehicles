The 2021 model has a larger front grille and wider air intakes for improved airflow, along with a more pronounced rear diffuser and a larger fixed rear wing for increased downforce. The 2021 GT4 also has updated headlight and taillight designs with more modern LED elements, giving it a slightly sharper, more contemporary look compared to the 2017.

While the overall shape remains similar, the 2021 GTS 4.0 has more defined lines and a slightly more angular appearance, giving it a fresher, more contemporary aesthetic.
